Civil War

2.5/5

This movie lacked meaning choosing to follow a theme of dreamed up violence. While the graphics in the movie was spectacular, I believe a fleshed out plot would have done wonders for this movie.

Twisters

2/5

In the remake of a film that came out less than 30 years ago, there was not much story to add. The characters seemed very far away from reality making it hard to root for them. However the twister chasing scenes and watching the lead character be a nerd about weather was pleasing.
