Ka - The Thief Next To Jesus

5/5

In The Thief Next To Jesus, Brownsville rapper Ka critiques sensaonalized hip hop culture and opts to celebrate the qualities of culture that he takes from such as loving God, family and your neighbor as your own. Mixed with amazing analogies and eloquent production this album is for anyone who believes in anything strongly and lovingly.

Earl Sweatshirt - Some Rap Songs

5/5

Mental health is not a game and grappling those issues publicly takes someone of strong character to embark gracefully. In Some Rap Songs, Earl Sweatshirt does exactly that. With songs of hope and lost hope Earl bares it all in a condense 30 minutes capturing a moment in time that leaves you wanting more.
